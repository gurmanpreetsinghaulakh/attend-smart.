Our Story

At Attend Smart, we understand the challenges teachers face in managing student attendance.

We know the excitement of teaching , but we also understand the challenges and stress that come with managing a classroom—because we've been there ourselves.

We're university students who understand the challenges our school teachers face with maintaining attendance on paper. That's why we developed an online attendance management system to simplify the process and make it more efficient.

At Attend Smart , We also have a deep understanding of the needs of different roles within a school. This ensures that teachers, administrators, and students get access to the right information, in the right format, at the right time—allowing everyone to focus on what they do best.

Our Goal

With this website, our goal is to eliminate the outdated paper-based system in schools. In a world where everything is going digital, why should attendance still rely on paper? By transitioning to our online platform, you can streamline the process, reduce errors, and save time. Join us in making your school's attendance system as modern and efficient as the rest of your educational tools.
